Welcome

This page collects ideas and suggestions from all students to help design our official class website. Your suggestions help shape how our class is seen, remembered, and enjoyed online. Everyone in the class can give ideas, and the final website will reflect your voices.

  • Suggestions are only for the class website.
  • All students can participate.
  • The final site will be based on what students suggest.

About This Suggestion Project

The goal is to build a website representing our S1 class, including vibes, jokes, photos, memories, achievements, and stories. Everyone contributes, rather than leaving decisions to a single person.

  • Help choose the best class name.
  • Decide sections and categories for the website.
  • Contribute ideas for content such as jazz, photos, events, and memories.
  • Every clean, respectful idea matters.

Serious, funny, or creative suggestions are all welcome—every student’s voice counts.
